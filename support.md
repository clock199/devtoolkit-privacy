## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Toolsmith work offline?**
Yes — all tools work completely offline with no internet connection required.

**How do I unlock Power Tools?**
Tap the Power Tools section in the tool list and follow the prompts to purchase the one-time unlock for $2.99.

**I purchased Power Tools but they are locked again after reinstalling.**
Tap the Power Tools section and use the Restore Purchase button to restore your purchase.

**What tools are included for free?**
Base64, SHA256, URL Encode, UUID Generator, Case Converter and Timestamp are all free.
